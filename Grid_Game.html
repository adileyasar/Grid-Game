Game Instructions

Welcome! Your objective is to click on the cells within the grid to gather experimental points. You have 10 clicks available to maximize your points, and you can repeatedly click on the same cell. What sets this experiment apart is the 'chain' method. Think of it as a sequence of participants who have previously played this game. The second player received the final solution of the first player, the third player got the last solutions of the second player, and this pattern continues. You will have the opportunity to see the last solution from the previous participant. Best of luck!
